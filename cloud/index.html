Culture of Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka's culture is a captivating blend of ancient traditions and vibrant celebrations. From the
rhythmic beats of traditional drumming to the aromatic allure of spicy curries, the island's
essence is woven into its rich history. With colorful festivals, historic temples, and warm hospitality,
exploring Sri Lankan culture is an enchanting journey into a world where tradition meets diversity.
