Nuwara Eliya



NUWARA ELIYA 165 KM From Colombo

Nuwara Eliya, often referred to as "Little England," captivates with its charming colonial architecture, lush tea plantations, and cool climate, offering visitors a refreshing escape amidst Sri Lanka's picturesque landscapes.

In the heart of the Indian Ocean lies a teardrop-shaped island, adorned with a myriad of wonders – this is Sri Lanka, and our story begins here.

Cloud Travels was born from a deep passion for exploration and a desire to share the hidden treasures of this enchanting land. Our journey started with a simple belief – that travel should be more than a destination; it should be an immersive experience, a voyage into the soul of a place.










As we navigated the diverse landscapes of Sri Lanka, we discovered the echoes of ancient civilizations in the crumbling stone walls of Polonnaruwa and the sacred city of Anuradhapura. The vibrant energy of Colombo's bustling markets and the serene beauty of Kandy's lush hills added vibrant strokes to our canvas.









Yet, Sri Lanka's allure goes beyond its physical beauty. It lies in the warmth of its people, the aromatic embrace of its cuisine, and the rhythmic beats of its traditional music. From the sun-kissed beaches of Mirissa to the misty tea plantations of Nuwara Eliya, every corner of this island resonates with a unique charm.



















Cloud Travels became more than a tourism company; it became a storyteller, weaving narratives of ancient temples, colonial heritage, and the daily lives of the people who call this island home. Our commitment is to guide you through these stories, allowing you to immerse yourself in the cultural tapestry of Sri Lanka.

Join us on this odyssey of discovery. Let Cloud Travels be your companion as you unravel the layers of Sri Lanka's timeless charm, where every journey is an opportunity to create your own story amid the whispers of history and the echoes of a vibrant culture.
